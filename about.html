Hello, my name is Dmitro Jovnyruk

I am a student at the Canadian Academy of Osteopathy, working towards my diploma of Master in Practice in Osteopathic Manipulative Sciences (M.OMSc). My goal is to always look for and find your health. That is, to not focus on the disease or problem, but ask why has it manifested and find how to liberate your body and allow it to restore natural equillibrium. With manual therapy, we connect your problems to structural dysfunctions and remove the roadblocks obstructing your natural ability to self-heal and self-regulate. The manual therapy I provide is gentle, but effective.

"Find it. Fix it. Leave it alone. Nature will do the rest." - Dr. Andrew Taylor Still (founder of Osteopathy)

With my concurrent principle's based manual osteopathic education and a variety of tools such as: myofascial release, muscle energy, craniosacral, and ligamentous articular release, I can able to help you rediscover the healthy you.
